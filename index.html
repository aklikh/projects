Svitlana Sholomitska and Ivan Kozik, creators of the 8komora

Author's artistic ceramics

8komora is a space where we create art for living next to you. Objects, be it plates, lighting or even a garbage can on the street, using clay, knowledge and feelings.

In 2014, Svitlana Sholomitska with Ivan Kozik, who put all this artistic mess in order, 8komora were created as a brand.

Upcicling Furniture

This direction began for us as a harmonious filling of our work and personal spaces. Things that, with the help of cleverness and love, do not replenish landfills, but fascinate with their peculiarity, history, and most importantly the idea that life gives more than one chance.
